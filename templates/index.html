About CIET Library

The CIET Library is the intellectual hub of our institution, offering thousands of books, journals, and digital resources to support academics and research. Our mission is to make knowledge accessible, engaging, and impactful for all.

Key Features

Vast Collection

Over 50,000+ books, journals, and reference materials across all disciplines.

Digital Library

Access thousands of e-books, research papers, and e-journals anytime, anywhere.

Smart Analytics

Track borrowing trends, top readers, and popular books with data-driven insights.
